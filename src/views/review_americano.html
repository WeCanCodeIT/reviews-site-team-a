Americano - A brief review of my favorite coffee drink

Espresso and Water, equal parts

Elegance combined with simplicity. This beverage is meant to be sweetened heavily, if you are European. However, as an American, I enjoy the bitterness this small drink provides. Most locations I frequent know how I take my coffee (small Americano, quad, and top it off with water). As I travelled some European cities, I had to learn how they take their coffee. Similar to mine, but with a lot more sugar!
